The Albums

Akan E.P
Listeners Thoughts
The beauty of the album, aside Akan’s wordplay is his ability to not only rap but rap well (he floats on the beats effortlessly) in an spectacular fashion, something the likes of Obrafour and Kwadei lay claim to. And the New York styled hip hop beatsreminiscent of the 90s matches with his flow. These two factors combine to seduce the listener first hand. CulArtBlog
Onipa Akoma
Listeners Thoughts
Authentic, Riveting, a Masterpiece; These are a few superlatives that best describe the debut album of rap artist Akan. Kwabena Da VIK!NG GhanaWeb
Akan on ‘Onipa Akoma’ has intelligently given us a project so dense and layered that it’d take weeks, months or years to unravel its essence. I won’t be mad if he never releases an album again. What he needs to do now is to let the corners of the country-and those outside- hear this beautiful piece of art. CulArtBlog
